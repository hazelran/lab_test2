Openings

We are always looking for passionate students who want to work at the intersection of video technology, deep learning, and international standardization.

Open Positions

We are currently recruiting:

  • Ph.D. students — interested in AI-based video coding, video quality enhancement, or VCM
  • M.S. students — passionate about video/image processing and coding standards
  • Undergraduate interns — eager to explore cutting-edge media technology research

What We Offer

  • Full tuition coverage through scholarships and RA/TA programs
  • Additional support for living expenses during project participation
  • Opportunities to collaborate with Samsung Electronics, LG Electronics, and other global IT companies on standardization projects
  • Travel support to attend international conferences (JVET, MPEG, ISCAS, ICME, etc.)
  • A collaborative, international lab environment

How to Apply

If you are interested, please send an email to aikiho@khu.ac.kr with:

  1. Your CV / résumé
  2. A brief statement of your research interests (1 paragraph)
  3. Relevant coursework or project experience in video/image processing, deep learning, or coding

We welcome applicants from all backgrounds. International students are very welcome.
