Principal Investigator

Prof. Aiahn Kiho Choi
Principal Investigator
aikiho@khu.ac.kr

Bio

Prof. Choi leads the Video Coding and Processing Lab (VCP-Lab) at Kyung Hee University. His research focuses on video coding standards, AI-based video compression and enhancement, and next-generation immersive media technology.

He has actively contributed to international standards bodies including MPEG (ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29) and JVET (Joint Video Experts Team), serving as Chairman of the Ad Hoc Group on Essential Video Coding (EVC) standard. In 2022, he received the Excellence Award from ISO and IEC in recognition of outstanding service to ISO/IEC JTC1/SC29.

VCP-Lab collaborates with industry partners including Samsung Electronics, LG Electronics, and Qualcomm, and has been supported by IITP (Institute of Information & Communications Technology Planning & Evaluation) research grants.

Research Interests

  • Video coding standards (VVC, HEVC, AVC, AV1, EVC)
  • AI/DNN-based video and image compression
  • Video Coding for Machines (VCM / FCVCM)
  • 4K/8K ultra-high-resolution and immersive video
  • International standardization (MPEG, JVET)
