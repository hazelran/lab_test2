About the Lab

VCP-Lab is committed to advancing video and image compression technologies in alignment with evolving international standards. We actively explore next-generation media including 4K/8K/16K video and immersive VR/AR content, leveraging deep learning and AI-based coding tools to anticipate the future of highly immersive media experiences.

Our lab maintains strong industry collaborations with global IT companies such as Samsung Electronics, LG Electronics, and Qualcomm, and actively participates in international standardization bodies including MPEG and JVET.

Research Themes

Video Coding Standards

VVC, HEVC, AVC, AV1, EVC — contributing to JVET and MPEG standard meetings.

AI-based Post-Processing

CNN/GAN/Transformer-based in-loop and post filters for compressed video quality enhancement.

Immersive & XR Media

High-resolution 4K/8K/16K, VR/AR/360° video processing and compression.

Video Coding for Machines

Feature coding and compression optimized for machine vision and AI analytics (VCM/FCVCM).
